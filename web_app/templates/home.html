Welcome To the GU Course Evaluation Dashboard

Problem:

Georgetown updates and publishes this large data set that reports course evaluation results for all the professors and classes at the school. However, this spreadsheet is hard to read and challenging to draw meaningful conclusions from.

Solution:

We aim to create an interactive search form and dashboard to make the results of the course evaluations easy for students to understand. This way, students will be able to make more informed decisions as to what professors they want to take, and don't have to rely on skewed rate my professor results. We envision that this will also help hold professors more accountable, as their course evaluations will be more public.

*Note: Evaluations from Fall of 2020 and Spring of 2021 are excluded since instruction was virtual during these semesters.
